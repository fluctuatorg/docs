Use this section to secure your account during onboarding. Complete these steps before you invite teammates and before you switch to Live Mode.

## Security setup checklist

1. [Set up 2FA](/account-security/set-up-2fa) on your own login first.
2. [Invite team members and assign permissions](/account-security/users-permissions) based on role.
3. [Create and manage API keys](/account-security/api-keys) only for technical users and systems.
4. [Restrict API access by IP](/account-security/ip-allowlist) when possible.
5. Follow [integration security best practices](/integration-best-practices/integration-security).

## Why this matters

- It reduces the risk of unauthorized access.
- It protects funds, payout operations, and customer data.
- It helps you move safely from Test Mode to Live Mode.

## Ongoing security habits

- Review team access regularly and remove accounts you no longer use.
- Rotate credentials when people change roles or leave your team.
- Monitor account activity and report suspicious behavior to [help@xendit.co](mailto:help@xendit.co).
