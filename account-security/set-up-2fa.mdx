---
title: "Set up 2FA"
---

Set up two-factor authentication (2FA) during onboarding, before you invite teammates or move to Live Mode.

2FA protects your account by requiring:

- Something you know: your password
- Something you have: a one-time code from your phone

## Supported 2FA methods

Xendit supports SMS and Time-based One-Time Password (TOTP) authenticator apps, including:

- Google Authenticator
- Authy
- Microsoft Authenticator

## Set up 2FA

1. Log in to your Xendit account.
2. Go to **Settings** > **Your Profile** > **2-factor Authentication** > **Setup 2FA**.
3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
4. Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
5. Save your recovery codes in a secure place separate from your phone.

## Recommendations

- Keep your recovery codes offline and secure.
- Enable 2FA for every teammate account.
- Set up 2FA on multiple devices for backup, if your policy allows it.
- Do not disable 2FA unless support instructs you to do so.

## Troubleshooting

- Code not working: Ensure your phone time is synced automatically.
- Lost device: Use your recovery codes to sign in, then set up 2FA again.
- Need help: Contact [help@xendit.co](mailto:help@xendit.co).
