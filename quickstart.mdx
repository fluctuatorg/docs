## Create and verify your account

You can start transacting after these two processes:

1. Sign up and fill in basic information. This creates your Xendit account and gives you immediate access to the dashboard in [Test Mode](/get-started/your-dashboard).
2. Verify your business to activate Live Mode and process real transactions.

You can integrate and run test transactions in Test Mode without verification.

### Sign up steps

1. Sign up [here](https://dashboard.xendit.co/register/).
2. Verify your email.
3. Fill in basic questions about your business.
4. Choose whether to activate your account right away or explore in Test Mode.

### Verify your business

1. In your dashboard, click **Verify Business** on the top banner.
2. Fill in your details and upload required documents:
   - Business documents for legal entity verification.
   - Identity documents for key stakeholders.
   - Proof of business such as your website, store photo, or social media links.
3. Wait for review. Verification usually takes `1-3` working days.

For detailed country-specific requirements, see [Verifying your account](/get-started/verification-requirements/verifying-your-account).
