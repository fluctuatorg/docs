---

Payouts helps you to send simple, fast, and secure transfers to all destinations across Xendit’s payout network.

### Key Benefits

- Efficiency: Process and send numerous payouts with a single API request, eliminating manual transactions and reducing administrative overhead.
- Cost-effectiveness: Minimize transaction fees and optimize operational costs by processing multiple payments concurrently.
- Reliability: Ensure timely and accurate payments with our robust and secure infrastructure.
- Flexibility: Send payments to various destinations and in multiple currencies, catering to diverse business needs.
- Transparency: Track payment status in real-time and gain complete visibility into your transactions.

### Use Cases

- Platform withdrawal: Xendit helps platforms to provide instant payouts from their users’ balances to the users’ bank accounts at any time.
- Marketplace escrow: Xendit’s flexible API enables marketplaces to forward verified payments from customers to merchants without any manual process.
- Payroll and commissions: Companies use our batch uploads to send payroll and commissions to their staff and agent partners easily. Paying unbanked workers is easy with our Payout to e-wallets.

## How it works

### Create a Payout

Create your payout to start sending funds to your recipient. Once successfully created, all you need to do is to sit tight and not wait as we process your request in real-time.

You have full control over how you want to start creating a payout. We provide both options of a fully-customizable API integration or an easy out-of-the-box Dashboard interface – depending on your needs and preference.

To get started, see [Batch Payouts via Dashboard](/send-money/payouts/payouts-2/batch-payouts-via-dashboard) or [Payouts API Integration](/send-money/integration-guide-2/integration-payouts).

### Track your Payout

Once your payout is created, we’ll process your payout in real-time and update you with any change of status, either `SUCCEEDED,` `FAILED`, or anything in between. You can subscribe to any status change events by subscribing to our webhook events, check your payout’s status through our `Get Payouts API` endpoints or directly from your dashboard.

Learn more about how Payout statuses change along the payout journey in [Payout Lifecycle](/send-money/payout-status-lifecycle).
