---
title: "Consolidated Reports"
---

From your Accounts Overview page, you can select one or more sub-accounts and generate either Balance or Transaction history reports to be sent to your email address.

1. Go to Accounts on your dashboard
2. Select the sub-accounts to export reports for
3. Click on Export and follow the instructions

You'll receive an email with a link to download the CSV report.

Note that there are currently some limitations:

- The reporting limit for the date range is 31 days
- If you are consolidating a large number of transactions across many sub-accounts, the report may exceed the limit and fail to generate:

  - Same-day exports (date range set to “Today”) are limited to 1 million rows
  - Exports for the previous day and older are limited to 10 million rows

## Exporting detailed reports for a sub-account

You can export Transaction or Balance history reports for a single account on the Activity page.

1. Go to the Accounts page
2. Choose a sub-account and navigate to the Account Activity page
3. Select the transaction type to export
4. Click CSV and follow the instructions

You will receive an email with a link to download the CSV report.
