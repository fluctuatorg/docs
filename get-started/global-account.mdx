A Xendit Global Account supports businesses with cross-border payments and operational needs, whether you're expanding into new markets, paying global suppliers, or managing a multi-country platform.

- If your business is registered outside of Indonesia, the Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, and Vietnam, a global account is automatically available to you.
- If your business is registered in Indonesia, the Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, or Vietnam and you are interested in using a global account, please contact [help@xendit.co](mailto:help@xendit.co) for assistance.

This account simplifies multi-country operations by providing a unified platform for payments, payouts, and balance management across various currencies and markets.

## Why use a Global Account?

Managing multiple payment accounts and dashboards for different business entities across various countries can lead to complex onboarding, separate agreements, and a fragmented financial overview.

The Xendit global account provides a single unified account, dashboard, and API key. This means less administrative overhead, a streamlined onboarding process, and a clear, consolidated view of all your payments, payouts, and balances across all supported currencies and markets.

## Key capabilities and benefits

The Xendit global account enables you to manage multi-currency operations through a single dashboard and API key.

- Multi-Currency Operations: Manage payments in various currencies and view multiple currency balances directly from your single dashboard.
- Global Collections: Collect payments in multiple markets and currencies with ease.
- Flexible Fund Management: Convert funds seamlessly between your different currency balances.
- Worldwide Payouts: Make payouts to customers, suppliers, or other third parties using Xendit's expanding global network.
- Simplified Settlement: Settle payments into your preferred home currency, streamlining your reconciliation process.

## API Key

Xendit offers different API key functionalities depending on your account type, catering to both local market operations and broader global reach. Understanding these differences is crucial for seamless integration and managing your transactions.

For Local Accounts:

If you operate a local Xendit account (e.g., exclusively for Indonesia, Philippines, etc.), your API keys are scoped to your specific market. This means:

- Market-Specific Usage: An API key generated for your Indonesian account can only be used for transactions within Indonesia (IDR currency, local payment methods, etc.). Similarly, an API key for a Philippine account is restricted to the Philippine market.
- Segregated Data: All payments, payouts, and other transactions processed using a local API key will be reflected only within the dashboard interface for that specific local market. This provides a clear, focused view of your operations in that region.

For Global Accounts (multi-currency support):

Xendit's Global Account is designed for businesses that operate across multiple countries and currencies, providing a unified experience. With a Global Account:

- Single API key for multiple markets: You will have one API key that can be used across all the various markets and currencies supported by your Global Account. This significantly simplifies your integration and management, as you don't need to manage separate keys for each country.
- Centralized interface: All your payments, acceptances, and payouts, regardless of the currency or market they originate from, will be consolidated and visible on a single Xendit dashboard interface. This provides a holistic view of your entire global financial operations, making reconciliation and reporting much more efficient.
- Multi-currency transactions: The single API key allows you to seamlessly add, accept, and send payments in multiple currencies supported by Xendit's Global Account, streamlining your cross-border transactions.

Important Note for Payment API Users:

It's important to be aware of this distinction from the outset. If you have a Global Account and use your single API key for transactions in different markets, all these transactions (both acceptances and payouts) will appear on one consolidated interface. This is a key benefit of the Global Account, designed to provide a unified overview of your multi-market operations. If you are accustomed to market-specific views, you will find all your data integrated into a single, comprehensive dashboard for your Global Account.
