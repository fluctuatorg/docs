title: "Prohibited and High Risk Industries & Regions"
---

> Guidelines:
>
> - For high-risk industries, merchants will go through an additional process and be required to provide additional documents as part of due diligence (e.g., a sectoral license).
> - For high-risk countries, merchants will go through an additional process for onboarding.
> - For prohibited industries and countries, merchants are not allowed to onboard.
> - Xendit Group collectively refers to all our entities and markets.
