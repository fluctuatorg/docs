Welcome to Xendit! We're a leading fintech company making money movement simple for businesses across Southeast Asia. With our comprehensive suite of APIs and no code tools, you can seamlessly accept payments, send payouts, and manage your business finances with ease.

Now, let's explore what Xendit has to offer and cover the basics to get you started.

## What can you do with Xendit?

- [Accept payments](/accept-payments/available-payment-products):

  - Boost your payments coverage and payment success rates with popular [local payment methods and cards schemes](/accept-payments/payment-channel-details/available-payment-channels) connected with Xendit.
  - Simplify your development with our simple APIs, off-the-shelf hosted payment page, subscription scheduler, platform plug-ins, or by using our no-code tool to create hosted payment pages.
- [Send money](/v1/docs/overview-4):

  - Send instant payouts to bank accounts or e-wallets across [Southeast Asia](/send-money/payout-channels/overview-payouts-coverage) with speed and reliability.
  - Send cross border payouts globally with ease and compliance.
- [Automate your processes](https://xendit-docs.document360.io/apidocs):

  - Integrate with Xendit through our APIs and SDKs. Our flexible tools allow you to fully automate your payment/ payout process, from user initiation to final reconciliation.
- [Operate across Southeast Asia](/get-started/overview#our-licenses):

  - Xendit's network allows you to accept payments and send payouts in multiple currencies across Indonesia, the Philippines, Malaysia, Thailand, and Vietnam. Our expertise will help you navigate the complex SEA environment.

## Getting started

1. [Create an account](/get-started/create-account): Sign up for a free Xendit account and complete the verification process.
2. Review our documentation: Familiarize yourself with our APIs, SDKs, and integration guides.
3. Choose your integration method: Pick the approach that best fits your technical needs.
4. Start accepting or sending money: Begin using Xendit to simplify your money movements!
