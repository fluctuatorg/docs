Getting started with Xendit is simple. You can start making transactions after completing the 2 processes below:

1. Sign up and fill in basic information: This will create your Xendit account and give you immediate access to our dashboard in [Test Mode](/get-started/your-dashboard). In Test Mode, you can explore our features, integrate with our APIs, and test transactions with virtual funds.
2. Verify your business: To accept real payments and make live transactions, you'll need to verify your business by submitting your details and documents. Once verified, you'll be able to switch to [Live Mode](/get-started/your-dashboard) and start accepting and sending money.

## How to sign up

1. Sign up [here](https://dashboard.xendit.co/register/)
2. Verify your email
3. Fill in basic questions about your business
4. You can choose to activate your account right away, or explore your dashboard in Test Mode

To process live transactions, you'll need to verify your business details. This essential step enables Live Mode, granting you full access to our complete suite of features.

## How to verify your business

1. On the banner at the top of your dashboard, click Verify Business
2. Fill in your business details and upload your documents. You will need 3 types of information to verify your business with Xendit

   1. Business Documents - Documents that provide necessary information of the legality of your business (e.g. Business Registration Document, Shareholder Structure, Business Licenses, etc.)
   2. Identity Documents - Documents required to verify the identity of stakeholders in your company
   3. Proof of Business Documents - Information to prove that your business matches the details submitted in the documents (e.g. website, photo of offline store, or social media links, etc.)
3. Once submitted, we will verify your details within 1-3 working days

## Verification process

Here’s what to expect:

1. Verification process

   - Our team will review the submitted documents and verify the information provided.
   - During this process if you need to update any documents submitted please contact help@xendit.co.
2. Approval or updates

   - Upon meeting all requirements, an approval email will be sent, and you’ll be able to start processing live transactions.
   - In some cases, additional information or document corrections may be needed. We will notify you via email with clear instructions for resubmission. You can directly resubmit the required documents via your dashboard through your email or homepage. For more details please see [here](https://help.xendit.co/hc/en-us/articles/15722666356633-How-to-Update-or-Resubmit-Information-For-Account-Activation-)
3. Rejection

   - If your account is deemed noncompliant to Xendit’s policies, your account may be rejected. For more information, you may reach out to our CS for more help on how to proceed. For a list of prohibited industries and countries please refer to [this page](https://docs.xendit.co/shared/a7832653-b836-4544-bef8-0da00b4c07e8).
4. Account suspension

   - If an account is found to be engaging in any prohibited activities (including but not limited to fraud, selling your Xendit account, etc.), the account will be suspended.
   - When the account is suspended, we will apply a money holding period to the outstanding balance. Withdrawals will also be restricted until after the holding period.

## Adding a new business account

![](/images/doc360/image-1732593007264.png)

To manage multiple businesses within Xendit (e.g. if you have offices in other countries), you can add new business accounts associated with your existing login credentials. Each new business account must undergo the verification process outlined above. After adding a new business, you can easily switch between your accounts using the Switch Business option in the dashboard menu.
