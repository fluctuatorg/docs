title: "Features"
---

Our Subscription features are designed to provide a robust and flexible recurring payment solution. The following table outlines the key capabilities, from comprehensive payment channel support and PCI-compliant card handling to automated notifications and retry mechanisms, ensuring a seamless experience for both you and your customers.

| Feature | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Payment channel coverage | Supports all payment channels that can be used for recurring payment. |
| PCI compliant card data collection using Xendit-hosted page | Tokenize your customer card details without the need to be PCI certified. Our Xendit-hosted checkout UI helps to handle sensitive card information collection. |
| Customer notifications | Automated email or WhatsApp notifications to your customer for subscription confirmations, missed payments, and successful transactions. |
| Retry mechanism | Automated retries to recover missed payments, improving collection rates. |
| Multiple payment methods | Support multiple payment methods to be linked to one subscriptions plan as main and backup payment method |
| Force subscription cycle attempt | Allows immediate charging for a specific subscription cycle. |
| Automated Payment Link for failed payments | Automatically send a Payment Link to your customer when a payment fails, allowing them to complete the payment for a specific cycle to recover the missed payment. |
