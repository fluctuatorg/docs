EasyStore is a powerful e-commerce platform designed to help businesses easily build and manage their online stores. It offers customizable themes, marketing tools, order management, and supports various payment methods — making it a great choice for sellers across Southeast Asia.

Follow this guide to start integrating Xendit with your EasyStore shop.

### Requirements

Before integrating Xendit with EasyStore, make sure you:

- Have an active EasyStore account (any plan is supported).
- Are registered with Xendit, and have access to your API key and Business ID.
- Have removed any IP whitelist settings from your Xendit Dashboard (if previously set).

### Installation

Follow these steps to start accepting payments on your EasyStore shop using Xendit:

[null](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/Screen%20Recording%202025-08-07%20at%2015.40.45.mov)

1. Log in to your Xendit Dashboard, and go to the Integrations tab.
2. Search for EasyStore under available plugins and click Install.
3. You’ll be redirected to EasyStore’s app marketplace to setup your Xendit account in EasyStore Admin Panel > Settings > Payments.
4. Select Xendit and configure your preferred payment channels (e.g., bank transfer, eWallets, cards).
5. Insert your `Xendit’s Business ID` as `Merchant ID`, and `Xendit’s Public API Key` as `Secret Key` in Easystore’s Xendit setup page
6. Click Save — you’re now ready to accept payments via Xendit.
