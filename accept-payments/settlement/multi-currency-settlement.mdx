---
title: "Multi-currency settlement (Beta)"
---

> This feature is currently only available for [Global Accounts](/get-started/global-account).

Xendit automatically converts all funds from payments into the default currency of your account. The default currency across all Xendit global accounts is USD.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeQsVFTaQoWMG-Y6iphC-svqtbWBiItiJzj54G6x275LL8sg2bBYZmEEE-FyQjQGCfb34PUp5ARhSeE7a196cIkshE_OG6ghA_YF1mXHNwRKq08a0i1VvF8DvHdcUnmG6Y9Rr3iRQ?key=-sCRALoqPGXPKm_M4fvtqw)

You may maintain balances in other currencies to reduce the need for currency conversions. When you open a currency balance, payments with a matching presentment currency will no longer be converted into your default currency, and will settle to that currency balance instead.

For example, if you are a Xendit user in Singapore with only a USD balance open, all payments with a different presentment currency (eg IDR, PHP) will be automatically converted and settled into that USD balance. When you add an IDR balance, any future IDR payments will automatically settle into the IDR balance without conversion.
