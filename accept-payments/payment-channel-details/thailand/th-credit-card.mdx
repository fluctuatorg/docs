The annual value of card transactions in the Thailand cards and payments market is estimated to be USD $62.5 billion in 2023 and will grow at a CAGR of more than 7% during 2023-2027.

About 30% of Thai cards are credit cards, the rest are ATM and Debit cards.   
Source: [Bank of Thailand](https://app.bot.or.th/BTWS_STAT/statistics/BOTWEBSTAT.aspx?reportID=685&language=ENG)

Credit card volumes have reached over 1 Billion USD in 2024 in Thailand and are expected to grow to even further in 2025.
