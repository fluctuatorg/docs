QR code payments in Thailand uses QR PromptPay, a Thailand QR code standard developed by BOT (Bank of Thailand).

---

## Payment flow

![](https://archive.docs.xendit.co/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fres.cloudinary.com%2Fdpvzoteik%2Fimage%2Fupload%2Fc_fill%2Ch_826%2Cw_2252%2FScreenshot_2024-04-17_at_14.22.49_iygfzk%3F_a%3DBATCr5fi0&w=3840&q=75)

  

1. On the checkout page, select QR PromptPay as your payment method
2. Screenshot the QR code that appears on your screen
3. Open your mobile banking or e-wallet app, then select from Gallery
4. Choose the screenshot that you’ve made containing the QR Code
5. Make sure the payment amount and merchant is correct
6. Confirm payment
