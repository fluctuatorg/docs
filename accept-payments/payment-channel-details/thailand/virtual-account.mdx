---
title: "Standard Chartered Virtual Account"
---

Standard Chartered Virtual Account is a payment channel provided by Standard Chartered, enabling customers to pay conveniently via unique virtual account numbers assigned to them.

Customers receive a virtual account number specific to them. They can complete the payment via mobile banking, internet banking, or ATM. Once the payment is made, you receive an instant confirmation, ensuring a seamless and secure payment experience for both you and your customers.

## Payment flow

![](/images/doc360/Virtual_Account_Step_1.jpg)

![](/images/doc360/Virtual_Account-Step2.jpg)

1. Click Transfer in your platform of choice (Mobile Banking, Internet Banking, ATM)
2. Select Standard Chartered as destination bank
3. Input your VA number
4. Input the amount to pay
5. Click confirm to transfer the payment
