Payment Flow

[null](https://cdn.document360.io/217abc43-8677-41fb-a81d-fceeb1fa0358/Images/Documentation/ShopeePay-TH%20Flow.mp4)

1. On the checkout page, end customers select ShopeePay E-Wallet
2. Redirect end customers to Shopee app to make the payment
3. End customers confirm the payment amount and merchant is correct
4. End customers click Pay to confirm the payment
