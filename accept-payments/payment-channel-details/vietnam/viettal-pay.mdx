Viettel Pay was launched by Viettel Group, one of Vietnam’s largest telecommunications companies, as a digital payment platform in 2018. Designed to provide accessible financial services, it offers features such as bill payments, money transfers, mobile top-ups, and QR code payments. Viettel Pay supports both online and offline transactions, catering to users in urban and rural areas alike.

Leveraging Viettel’s extensive network and reputation, Viettel Pay has rapidly gained a large user base. Its integration with government services and partnerships with merchants nationwide have further cemented its position as a trusted and versatile digital payment solution in Vietnam.
