ZaloPay is a popular digital wallet and payment platform in Vietnam, offering users a seamless way to pay for goods and services, transfer money, and top up mobile accounts. Integrated with Zalo, Vietnam’s leading messaging app, ZaloPay provides a highly convenient and trusted payment solution, leveraging a vast user base to facilitate secure and efficient transactions across the country.

When checking out with BCA VA, customers receive a virtual account number specific to their transaction. They can complete the payment via mobile banking, internet banking, or ATM. Once the payment is made, you receive an instant confirmation, ensuring a seamless and secure payment experience for both you and your customers.
