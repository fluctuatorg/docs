![](/images/doc360/akulaku-logo.svg)Akulaku is a leading banking and digital finance platform in Southeast Asia, with a strong presence in Indonesia. Founded in 2016, Akulaku started as a consumer lending platform and has since expanded its services to include e-commerce, digital banking, and wealth management. Akulaku aims to provide accessible financial services to underserved customers in emerging markets, particularly those who lack access to traditional banking.

The platform offers a range of products, including virtual credit cards, installment loans, and online shopping, all accessible through its mobile app. Akulaku's innovative approach to financial services has made it a popular choice for Indonesians seeking convenient and affordable financial solutions.
