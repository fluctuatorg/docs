AstraPay is an emerging digital payment solution from one of Indonesia's largest business conglomerates. AstraPay is developed by Astra International, leveraging its extensive automotive and financial services network to provide integrated digital payment solutions across its ecosystem of services and beyond.

When customers select AstraPay at checkout, they can complete the payment either by logging into their AstraPay account directly on your checkout page, or by opening their AstraPay app and authorizing the payment through the app's interface. The payment is confirmed instantly once the customer validates the transaction using their AstraPay PIN or biometric authentication.
