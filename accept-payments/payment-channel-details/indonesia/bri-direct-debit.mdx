title: "BRI Direct Debit"

![](/images/doc360/image_64_.png)

BRI Direct Debit is a convenient automated payment method from Indonesia's largest bank by assets. BRI Direct Debit is offered by Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI), a state-owned bank with the largest network in Indonesia, serving millions of customers from urban centers to rural areas.

When customers choose BRI Direct Debit, they authorize automatic deductions from their BRI account for payments. During checkout, customers need to authenticate using their BRI credentials and approve the direct debit mandate. Once the mandate is approved, payments are automatically processed on the due date, and you receive instant confirmation when the transaction is completed.
