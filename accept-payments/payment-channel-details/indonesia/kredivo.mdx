## Payment flow

<iframe src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Lxzi9eULYH8" title="" className="w-full rounded-xl" height="360"></iframe>

1. On the checkout page, select PayLater > Kredivo
2. Fill in customer details and continue
3. Choose installment plan on the Kredivo payment page
4. Log in to Kredivo with your phone number and PIN, or create a new account
5. Enter the OTP sent to your phone and click Bayar (Pay)
