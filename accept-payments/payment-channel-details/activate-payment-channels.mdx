### Activation methods (applicable after [account verification](https://xendit-docs.document360.io/docs/create-account#how-to-verify-your-business))

- Instant - Available immediately after your account has been verified
- Dashboard - Available by request on Xendit dashboard
- Contact CS - Available by request to help@xendit.co

## How to activate payment channels on Xendit dashboard

1. Log in to your [Xendit dashboard](https://dashboard.xendit.co/)
2. Click Payment Channels on your sidebar
3. Click Activate Payment Channels
4. Select the payment channels you’d like to activate
5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the process

> Note:
>
> - Some payment channels may require additional documentation or verification
>
>   - Kredivo requires an additional User Acceptance Testing (UAT) process after you submit an activation request. You’ll need to complete this UAT to accept Kredivo transactions. Please refer to [Kredivo UAT](/accept-payments/integration-guide/payment-channel-uat/kredivo-uat) for detailed steps.
> - Some payment channels might not be available for certain industries
