title: "ShopeePay"
---

ShopeePay is an e-wallet service offered by Shopee, one of the leading online shopping platforms in Malaysia. Users can link their Malaysian bank accounts to their ShopeePay profiles, allowing them to make seamless online and offline payments.

ShopeePay is increasingly popular in Malaysia, with millions of active users utilizing it monthly, making it a vital payment method for merchants aiming to connect with Malaysian shoppers. You can enable ShopeePay payments on your website to accommodate users accessing via desktop or mobile browsers.

## Payment flow

For desktop users, the payment process includes displaying a QR code on the checkout page that users can scan using their ShopeePay app to complete the purchase. This setup is straightforward to implement once you add ShopeePay to your Customer Area.

For mobile users, direct them to their ShopeePay app to finalize their payment, after which they are redirected back to your website's payment page.
