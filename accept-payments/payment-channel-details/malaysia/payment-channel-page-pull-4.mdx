title: "GrabPay"
---

GrabPay is a digital wallet developed by Grab, the largest ride-sharing service in Southeast Asia, which has also branched into delivery, mobile payments, and financial services. The app, which serves over 100 million users, allows shoppers to link their credit or debit cards to their GrabPay wallet for transactions.

## Payment flow

To make a payment with GrabPay, users are redirected from your website or app to the GrabPay login page. They authenticate themselves using their phone number and a one-time password (OTP) sent to their phone by Grab. After authentication, users can choose from the available payment methods and confirm their transaction.
