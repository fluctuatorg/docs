Maya (formerly known as PayMaya) is one of the most popular digital payment platforms in the Philippines, offering a comprehensive suite of financial services, including mobile wallet payments, virtual and physical cards, and banking features through Maya Bank. It is a highly trusted payment method for both online and offline transactions, with millions of active users across the country.

Maya's versatility makes it a preferred choice among consumers and businesses. Customers can use Maya for a wide range of transactions, from online shopping and bill payments to money transfers and QR payments in physical stores. For businesses, integrating Maya as a payment option means access to a growing user base and the ability to offer a cashless, seamless payment experience.
