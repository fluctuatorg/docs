Cebuana Lhuillier is a popular payment method in the Philippines, offering over 2,500 branches nationwide. Cebuana Lhuillier offers a reliable, cash-based payment option for customers across the Philippines, making it an ideal choice for businesses targeting cash-paying consumers.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest Cebuana Lhuillier branch before payment code is expired
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a Bills Payment to XENDIT
3. You will receive a `Transaction Form Slip`

   Transaction Form Slip

   Please fill out the following information:

   1. Transaction Type: Payment
   2. Sender: Your Name
   3. Receiver: Xendit (for Xendit biller) / Merchant Name (for Merchant biller)
   4. Control No./ Account No./ Reference No: Payment Code given by merchant
   5. Amount: Amount due of your payment code

   ![](/images/doc360/Screenshot_2025-01-02_at_17.12.48.png)
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only)
5. Keep the payment proof from Cebuana
6. Payment has successfully completed
