QRPh is the national Quick Response (QR) code standard in the Philippines for real-time payments. It allows customers to make instant payments from their bank or e-wallet apps by scanning a QR code displayed by a merchant.

## How to pay

Find the QR Code

1. Select QRPh as your payment method on the checkout page
2. A QR code will be displayed along with the transaction details (e.g., amount and merchant name)

Payment Details

1. Open your mobile banking app or e-wallet that supports QRPh
2. Use the Scan QR Code feature in your app
3. Point your phone camera at the QR code displayed on the checkout page
4. Verify the transaction details (e.g., amount and recipient)

Confirm Payment

1. Confirm the transaction in your app
2. Enter your PIN or use biometric authentication (if required)
