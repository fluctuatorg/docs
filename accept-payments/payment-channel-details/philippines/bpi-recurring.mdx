The Bank of the Philippine Islands (BPI) is one of the largest and most trusted banks in the Philippines, with a strong presence in both traditional and digital banking services. On top of BPI direct debit that serves one-time payment use cases, BPI also offers a recurring solution for tokenized / recurring payment flows. This is widely used for regular payment use cases such as subscriptions, utilities, loans, tuition fees, and instalment plans.

This simplifies payment management by eliminating the need for manual payment processing, making it highly popular among businesses and consumers alike. Businesses benefit from reliable and timely payment collection, while customers enjoy the convenience of automated transactions without worrying about missing deadlines.
