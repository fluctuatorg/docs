![](/images/doc360/USSC_Logo.svg)

USSC (Universal Storefront Services Corporation) provides a convenient way for customers to pay for online purchases offline by generating a unique reference number that can be presented at any of their numerous branches. This allows businesses to accept cash payments from customers who may not have access to online banking or prefer traditional payment methods.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest branch USSC branch before payment code expires. You may refer to this [branch locator](https://ussc.com.ph/branches/).
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a payment for Dragonpay
3. You will be given a `Bayad Form` to fill-up

   Bayad Form

   Please fill out the following information:

   1. Biller: Dragonpay
   2. Account/Ref No: Payment code given by merchant
   3. Account Name: Your Name
   4. Bill Amount: Amount due of your payment code

   ![](/images/doc360/Screenshot_2025-01-05_at_17.12.14.png)
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment(cash only)
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment  
   ![](/images/doc360/Screenshot_2025-01-05_at_10.04.22.png)
