LBC Express is a Philippine courier company offering a wide range of services, including package delivery, money remittance, and cargo shipping, with an extensive domestic and international network. LBC, with its extensive network of branches across the Philippines, allows customers to pay for online purchases in cash by presenting a transaction reference number at any LBC location.

## How to pay

1. Visit your nearest LBC branch before payment code expires
2. Tell the cashier/staff that you will be making a PayCollect payment to XENDIT
3. You will receive a `Transaction Form` Slip

   Transaction Form

   Please fill out the following information:

   1. Date: Date of transaction
   2. Name: Your name
   3. Mobile Number: Your mobile number
   4. Amount to Send: Amount due of your payment code
   5. Write your payment code at the upper right of the form (same field of Date)

      ![](/images/doc360/Screenshot_2025-01-05_at_09.53.40.png)
4. Give the filled out slip to the cashier/staff with your payment (cash only)
5. You will receive a receipt as proof of payment  
   ![](/images/doc360/Screenshot_2025-01-05_at_09.55.46.png)
