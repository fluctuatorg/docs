7-Eleven in the Philippines offers a convenient, cash-based payment option for online shoppers. With over 3,000 stores nationwide, it provides an easy way for customers to pay for online purchases, particularly for those without bank accounts or credit cards.

1. End-customer to select 7-ELEVEN on the checkout page link provided by merchant
